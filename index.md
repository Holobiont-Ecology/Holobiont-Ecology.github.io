Welcome to Lee Lab.

We study **the organizing principles of holobionts**, **symbiotic communication**, and **multi‑species chronobiology**.

Our work combines systems biology, microbial ecology, and mycorrhiza genetics to uncover **the rules that govern host–microbiome interactions across space and time**.

Our long‑term goal is to translate the principles of holobiont organization into **strategies for ecological conservation**, **sustainable agriculture**, and **the design of resilient host–microbiome systems under environmental change**.
