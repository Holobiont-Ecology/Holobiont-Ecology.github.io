--- layout: splash title: "Holobiont Systems Biology Lab" permalink: / --- Welcome to Lee Lab. We study the organizing principles of holobionts, symbiotic communication, and multi‑species chronobiology. Our work combines systems biology, ecological modelling, and mycorrhiza genetics to uncover the rules that govern plant–microbiome interactions across space and time. Our long‑term goal is to translate the principles of holobiont organization into strategies for ecological conservation, sustainable agriculture, and the design of resilient plant–microbiome systems under environmental change.
